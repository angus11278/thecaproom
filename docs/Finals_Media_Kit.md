# THE CAP ROOM: FINALS LAUNCH MEDIA KIT (draft)

## The pitch, one paragraph
A new independent publication audits football against the only referee that never barracks: the market. Built on 14,915 games, 130 years of archives and $429M of exchange volume, The Cap Room publishes what clubs own (skill, priced) and what they merely borrowed (luck, mean-reverting), with a methodology open enough to attack and a public log of its own killed findings.
